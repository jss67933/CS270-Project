    What is User Experience?

    As part of introducing ourselves, college student are often asked what their major or minor is. When I list my major and certifactes I'm currently in progress people often wonder what "User Experience" even means? I feel it is nesscary to introduce what User Experience is and why it is important before further introducing works I have created related to this field of study.

    "User experience is how a user interacts with and experiences a product, system or service. It includes a person's perceptions of utility, ease of use, and efficiency." - Wikipedia

    "the overall experience of a person using a product such as a website or computer application, especially in terms of how easy or pleasing it is to use." - Oxford Language Dictionary

    User experience design is creating products that incorporate the products' image, design, and functionality, while also factoring the user’s satisfaction and perception of the product.
